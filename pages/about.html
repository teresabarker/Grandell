About Me


i'm teresa, but most people call me terry

Hey, I’m Terry, a 24-year-old software developer and content creator.

My story starts back on Tumblr in 2013 by editing the HTML of my pre-made theme to have a cute cursor or to make it snow during the winter months (of course). That was my first real coding experience.

Flash forward to today, and I’ve been working full-time as a software engineer for 2+ years and have over 7,000 followers and 1.8 Million likes on TikTok. I’ve gotten the opportunity to work with brands like Bondi Sands, Grande Cosmetics, and more.

This site has transformed a lot since I first started it in 2018 with a free WordPress theme to where it is today which is 100% designed and developed by me.

Whether you're coming from social media, are a brand looking to collaborate, or are a company looking for my portfolio, welcome and enjoy ~

much love, terry
