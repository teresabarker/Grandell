About Me


i'm teresa, but most people call me terry

Hey I’m Terry, a 24-year-old who enjoys coding, writing, and creating things.

My story starts back on Tumblr in 2013 with editing the HTML of a theme I downloaded to have a cute cursor or to make it snow (obviously).

That's honestly what got me into coding. It's been a long journey from there to now having worked in industry as a software engineer for 3 years. Although, I still Google things just as much.

This site mostly serves as a landing zone for all of my projects, coding-related and not. It actually used to be more of a blog, but now that I do most of my writing offline, it's much more image-focused and about curating an aesthetic - again, not much has changed from my Tumblr days.

Whether you're here to check out my portfolio, a friend poking around, or me in the future reading this (hey, future me) welcome and enjoy.

much love, terry
