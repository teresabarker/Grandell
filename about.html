About Me


i'm teresa, but most people call me terry

Back in 2013, I got my first real taste of “coding” by editing the HTML of a theme I downloaded onto my Tumblr. I spent hours changing around the colors and playing around with different cursors to find the cutest one. The people that created these themes are so smart, I thought. How cool if that could be me?

Flash forward to today, and 13-year-old me wouldn’t believe that she now has her own website that is 100% designed and developed by her. Not to mention going to college for it, graduating Summa Cum Laude, and becoming a full-time software engineer.

Once an idea roots itself in my mind and I’m able to visualize it, it becomes a dream of mine to see it through. First with this website you’re on now, and then with every other project I’ve done and will continue to dream up in the future.

So, that’s really what this website is - a living, breathing portfolio for my Portfolio. Whether you’re here to check that out or simply stopping by, thank you and enjoy.

much love, terry
